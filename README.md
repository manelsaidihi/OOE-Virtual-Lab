## Physics Formulas Used

### Optical Power
P = 1 / f'

### Thin Lens Equation (Descartes)
1/OA' − 1/OA = 1/f'

### Lateral Magnification
M = OA' / OA

### Image Height
A'B' = M × AB

### Snell's Law
n₁ sin(θ₁) = n₂ sin(θ₂)

---

## Problem Summary

A diverging lens (f' = −10 cm, P = −10 D) is placed in a door peephole.  
A guard of height AB = 1.80 m stands at OA = −50 cm from the lens.

| Question | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Q1 – Optical Power | P = −10 D |
| Q2 – Image Position | OA' = −8.33 cm (virtual) |
| Q3 – Magnification | M = +0.167 (erect, reduced) |
| Q4 – Image Height | A'B' = 30 cm |
| Q5 – Field of View | Widened by angular compression |
